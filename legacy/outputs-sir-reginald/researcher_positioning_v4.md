## The Demo Script (2 Minutes)

### Perfect Flow with Timestamps

```
[0:00-0:10] CONTRAST SETUP
Silent generic safety app on screen.
"This is what exists today. Upload a photo. Wait for analysis. Hope you didn't already hurt yourself."

[0:10-0:25] INTRODUCE SIR REGINALD
"Now meet Sir Reginald Makesworth III - your distinguished workshop guardian."
Sir Reginald appears, surveys camera.
"Good day! I shall be keeping a watchful eye on your workshop. Might you show me about?"

[0:25-0:45] GUIDED CAMERA SETUP (Innovation Moment)
User pans camera as instructed.
"Ah yes, there's the table saw... angle down a touch so I might see your hands clearly... Splendid!"
(Shows AI DIRECTING the human - memorable, unique)

[0:45-1:05] SAFE WORK DEMONSTRATION
User works safely. Sir Reginald gives encouraging commentary.
"Excellent form. Proper grip. Safety glasses in place. Do carry on!"
(Establishes baseline - he's watching, he approves)

[1:05-1:25] SAFETY GLASSES INTERVENTION
User removes safety glasses, reaches toward tool.
"I say, before we proceed - might I trouble you to don your safety spectacles?"
User complies. "Much obliged. One's eyes are rather difficult to replace."

[1:25-1:45] THE SHOUT (Peak Drama)
User's hand drifts toward spinning blade.
"[NAME]! HAND!"
User flinches, pulls back.
Calmer: "Apologies for the outburst. But I did notice your fingers approaching the blade rather more closely than I'd prefer."
(THE MOMENT - judges remember this)

[1:45-2:00] CLOSING
User completes task safely.
"Splendid work! Another day, another digit saved. Do carry on with your excellent work. Cheerio!"
```
