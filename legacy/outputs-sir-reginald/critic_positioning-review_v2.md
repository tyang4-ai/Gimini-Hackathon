These MUST be real numbers before submission. Placeholders communicate "we haven't actually tested this." Real numbers communicate "we built it and proved it works."

**Priority: CRITICAL**

A skeptical judge might ask: "Why workshops specifically? Seems niche."

The positioning doc touches on broader applications (kitchens, construction, labs) but buries it. Consider moving the "Impact" framing earlier - workshop is the PROOF OF CONCEPT, not the entire vision.

A real testimonial hits different than claimed metrics. Even 30 minutes with one real maker transforms credibility.

**Priority: HIGH** (This is in the doc as "To Be Captured" - ACTUALLY DO IT)
