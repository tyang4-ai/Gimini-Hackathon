### 1.2 What Research Says About Live vs. Delayed Feedback

From academic sources:

> "Real-time feedback delivered during practice helps speakers make quick adjustments, stay flexible, and build confidence right in the moment." - Feedback Strategies for Continuous Speaking Improvement

**The nuance:** Post-hoc feedback is better for learning. Real-time coaching is better for performance. In high-stakes moments (investor pitch, live TV), you can't stop to reflect - you need in-the-moment course correction.

## Part 2: The Gap We're Exploiting

### 2.1 The "Post-Hoc Problem"

Current tools work like this:
1. You record/practice your presentation
2. After you finish, AI analyzes it
3. You get feedback: "At 1:32, you said 'um' too much"
4. You try again

**The problem:** By the time you see the feedback, the moment is gone. You can't go back to 1:32 and fix it in real-time.

### 2.2 The "Whisper-In-Ear" Opportunity

Imagine instead:
1. You're presenting live
2. At 1:32, as you start to fumble, a voice whispers: "Slow down, you've got this"
3. You recover in the moment
4. At 2:15, the AI notices the audience's attention dropping (you're looking at your notes)
5. It whispers: "Eyes up - engage the room"

**This is what professional speaking coaches do.** They sit in the back of the room and give real-time hand signals or earpiece coaching. We're automating that coach.

### 2.3 Adjacent Domains with Same Gap

The "post-hoc vs real-time" gap exists across multiple high-stakes domains:

| Domain | Current AI State | Real-Time Opportunity |
|--------|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Pitch coaching** | Post-hoc feedback | MID-PITCH energy/body language coaching |
| **Interview prep** | Turn-based roleplay | MID-ANSWER confidence coaching |
| **Negotiation training** | Script generation | MID-NEGOTIATION strategy whispers |
| **Media training** | Post-interview review | MID-INTERVIEW presence coaching |
| **Debate practice** | Argument analysis | MID-DEBATE counterpoint suggestions |
| **Executive presence** | Workshop feedback | MID-MEETING authority coaching |

### Concept 1: PresenceCoach - Real-Time Executive Communication Coach

**The Broader Pitch:** Instead of "pitch coaching" (narrow use case), we expand to "any high-stakes communication moment."

**Problem Statement:**
Executives spend 80% of their time communicating (meetings, presentations, negotiations, 1:1s). Executive presence training costs $5,000-15,000 per day. Most leaders never receive real-time feedback on HOW they communicate - only WHAT they communicated. The 7-38-55 rule shows 93% of communication effectiveness comes from tone and body language, yet most coaching focuses only on words.

**Target User:**
Professionals preparing for high-stakes communication: investor pitches, board presentations, difficult conversations, media appearances, negotiation meetings.

**Why Gemini Live is ESSENTIAL:**
- Continuous video analysis of body language, eye contact, posture
- Real-time voice interruption (not text alerts)
- Context-aware coaching (knows what you're presenting about)
- Proactive intervention (decides WHEN to coach, not just responding to prompts)

**Core Features:**
1. **Scenario Selection** - Pitch, interview, negotiation, media appearance, difficult conversation
2. **Live Video Analysis** - Tracks eye contact, posture, gestures, facial expressions
3. **Whisper Coaching** - Voice prompts only you hear: "Slow down", "More energy", "Stand taller"
4. **Energy Graph** - Real-time visualization of your presence/confidence level
5. **Context Integration** - Upload your deck/notes so AI understands WHAT you're presenting
6. **Q&A Simulation** - AI plays skeptical audience, coaches through tough questions

**Demo Scenario (3 minutes):**
1. (0:00-0:15) Intro: "Every leader needs real-time feedback. No one gets it."
2. (0:15-0:45) User begins pitch. PresenceCoach whispers: "Strong opening. Maintain that energy."
3. (0:45-1:15) User gets to numbers slide, voice drops, looks at notes
4. (1:15-1:20) PresenceCoach: "Energy dip detected. Eyes up, own the numbers."
5. (1:20-1:40) User recovers, delivers with confidence
6. (1:40-2:00) PresenceCoach (as skeptical investor): "Your burn rate seems high."
7. (2:00-2:20) User answers nervously, looks down
8. (2:20-2:25) PresenceCoach: "Good answer. Now say it again with direct eye contact."
9. (2:25-2:40) User delivers with confidence
10. (2:40-3:00) Show energy graph overlay: "Here's where you peaked and dipped"

**"Wow Factor" Hook:**
"Imagine a world-class executive coach whispering in your ear during your most important moments - telling you exactly when to slow down, stand taller, or own your numbers. That's PresenceCoach."

### Concept 2: NegotiationWhisper - Real-Time Deal Coaching

**The Niche Play:** Focus on one of the highest-stakes, lowest-support domains - live negotiations.

**Problem Statement:**
Two out of three professionals don't negotiate salary, potentially losing $1 million over their career (PON research). **Target User:**
Professionals facing salary negotiations, contract discussions, vendor negotiations, or any high-stakes deal conversation.

**Why Gemini Live is ESSENTIAL:**
- Analyzes both YOUR body language AND verbal cues in real-time
- Provides strategic whispers based on negotiation dynamics
- Context-aware (knows what you're negotiating for, your BATNA, your target)
- Can intervene when it detects you're about to make a mistake (saying yes too quickly, showing desperation)

**Core Features:**
1. **Pre-Call Briefing** - Input your target, walkaway, BATNA, counterparty info
2. **Live Strategy Whispers** - "They seem open to moving. Push for 10% more." / "You're speaking too fast - they might sense desperation."
3. **Body Language Alerts** - "You nodded before hearing the full offer - stay neutral"
4. **Counteroffer Suggestions** - Real-time alternatives based on conversation flow
5. **Post-Negotiation Debrief** - Replay with annotations showing missed opportunities

**Demo Scenario (3 minutes):**
1. (0:00-0:15) Setup: "Most people lose thousands by NOT negotiating. NegotiationWhisper changes that."
2. (0:15-0:30) User receives offer: "$95,000"

4. (0:35-0:50) Silence. Counterparty adds: "We could also discuss signing bonus."
5. (0:50-1:00) NegotiationWhisper: "Good. Now counter with your target range."
6. (1:00-1:30) User negotiates. NegotiationWhisper provides micro-coaching throughout.
7. (1:30-2:00) Tough objection. User's body language shows defeat.
8. (2:00-2:05) NegotiationWhisper: "Sit up. They haven't said no. Try: 'What would need to be true for you to offer X?'"

10. (2:30-3:00) Close with outcome: "Average negotiation improvement: 15% better outcomes"

**"Wow Factor" Hook:**
"What if you had a world-class negotiation coach whispering strategy in your ear during the actual negotiation? NegotiationWhisper reads the room, watches your body language, and tells you exactly when to pause, when to push, and when to walk away."
