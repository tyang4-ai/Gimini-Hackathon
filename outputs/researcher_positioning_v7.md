```
[0:00-0:05] HOOK

"Before, not after. He caught it before I completed the action."

"Sir Reginald isn't just watching for danger - he's documenting everything."

Show complete tutorial with timestamps.
"A complete tutorial. All written by an AI that actually watched me work."
```

### Why This Order Works

1. **Safety first:** The SHOUT is the hook. Lead with the memorable moment.
2. **Documentation as twist:** After judges think they've seen the product, reveal unexpected depth.
3. **"But wait, there's more":** The twist structure makes judges feel they're discovering something.
4. **Tangible output:** The documentation is PROOF that the marathon context works.
