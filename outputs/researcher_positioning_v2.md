# WorkshopCopilot Positioning Statement v2

## Primary Positioning

**For:** Home workshop hobbyists who work alone - the maker in the garage at midnight, the weekend woodworker, the 3D printing enthusiast with no one to ask

**Who:** Risk injury with no one watching and waste hours diagnosing failures with no one to help

**The:** WorkshopCopilot

**Is:** The only AI that watches your physical workspace in real-time - your 24/7 shop mentor

**That:** Proactively warns you about safety hazards before accidents happen and visually diagnoses problems when you show it failed prints, bad cuts, or equipment issues

**Unlike:** The current reality where makers work alone with no safety oversight, wait hours for forum responses when projects fail, and have no one to ask at 2 AM when they're finally getting to their project

**It (Primary Differentiators):** Is the only AI that can stop you BEFORE an accident - because it sees the danger developing in real-time. Not after you upload a photo. Not after you describe the problem. Before. Enabled by Gemini Live's continuous video API with natural voice interaction, so your hands never leave your work.

### The Two-Feature Focus

**Feature 1: Safety Monitor (Proactive)**
- Watches continuously via webcam
- Speaks up WITHOUT being asked
- Catches: missing PPE, dangerous hand positions, improper technique, environmental hazards
- Voice: Calm, direct, non-judgmental - like an experienced peer, not a nagging system
- *"I notice you're reaching toward the blade without your safety glasses on..."*

**Feature 2: Visual Troubleshooter (Reactive)**
- Activated when user asks
- Analyzes what it sees in real-time
- Handles: 3D print failures, laser cutter issues, basic woodworking problems, material identification
- Voice: Diagnostic, specific, actionable - like a mentor who's seen this before
- *"I can see layer separation here, which typically means..."*

---

### Tools We Support (Scope)

**In Scope:**
- 3D Printers (FDM focus): Bed leveling, adhesion, stringing, layer issues, supports
- Laser Cutters: Power/speed settings, material selection, safety protocols, cut quality
- Basic Woodworking: Table saws, miter saws, drills - safety and basic technique
- Material Identification: Common woods, plastics, composites by visual inspection
- Design Tools: Inkscape (vectors), Tinkercad (simple 3D), Cura/PrusaSlicer (slicing)

**Explicitly Out of Scope:**
- Complex CAD (Fusion 360, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Rhino)
- CNC machining
- Welding
- Electronics/soldering
- Advanced manufacturing equipment

**Why This Scope:**
We're not trying to replace a master machinist. We're the safety net and instant helper for the hobbyist working alone at 2 AM. Deep expertise in common maker tools beats shallow coverage of everything.

### Why Gemini Live (Technical Foundation)

**What Gemini Live Enables:**
- Continuous video streaming (not single image analysis)
- Sub-second response latency for safety-critical moments
- Natural voice interaction (hands-free operation)
- Multi-modal understanding (sees AND hears the workshop)

**Why This Matters for WorkshopCopilot:**
The AI needs to catch dangerous situations faster than a human can complete a dangerous action. That requires continuous awareness, not upload-analyze-respond cycles. Gemini Live is the first consumer-accessible API that makes this possible.

**What Was Hard:**
Maintaining real-time video streaming while keeping response latency low enough for safety intervention. The gap between "sees danger" and "speaks warning" has to be shorter than the time to complete a dangerous motion.
